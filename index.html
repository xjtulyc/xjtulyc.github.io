About Me

Research in AI for Medical Diagnosis

I am a PhD candidate at the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Liwei Wang.

My research focuses on the application of machine learning and computer vision in biomedical engineering. I am particularly interested in developing AI-powered medical diagnosis systems that can make healthcare more accessible and accurate.

Research Interests

Machine Learning Computer Vision Medical AI Deep Learning Biomedical Engineering Medical Image Analysis
Mission: Develop artificial intelligence medical diagnosis systems for social good.
